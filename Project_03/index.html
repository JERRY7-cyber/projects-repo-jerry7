About Robots

Robots are intelligent machines created to perform tasks that would normally require human effort, and over time they have become an important part of modern life. From their early beginnings as simple mechanical devices, robots have evolved into highly advanced systems powered by computer technology, sensors, and artificial intelligence. They are used in many fields today, such as manufacturing, where they build products with speed and accuracy; healthcare, where they assist doctors in performing delicate surgeries; space exploration, where rovers investigate distant planets; and even in households, where service robots clean floors or provide assistance. The greatest advantage of robots is their ability to work in places or conditions that may be too dangerous, repetitive, or complex for people, which makes them both practical and valuable. As technology continues to grow, robots are becoming more interactive and capable of learning, which opens up even greater possibilities for the future, while also raising important questions about how they will change jobs, education, and human society as a whole.
